<p><p>Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems are categorized primarily into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ERP offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, enabling organizations to reduce infrastructure costs and enhance collaboration. In contrast, on-premises ERP requires installation on local servers, providing greater control and customization but often involves higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities. Each type serves different business needs, with cloud solutions favoring agility and on-premises systems catering to those needing extensive control and security.</p></p>
