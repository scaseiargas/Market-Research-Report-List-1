<p><p>The IT infrastructure monitoring market consists of two primary types: on-premise and cloud/hosted solutions. On-premise monitoring involves deploying software and hardware within a company's own data centers, providing full control over data management but requiring significant upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud/hosted monitoring utilizes third-party vendors, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs while facilitating remote access. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs, budget, and security requirements.</p></p>
