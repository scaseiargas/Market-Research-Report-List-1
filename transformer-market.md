<p><p>The transformer market is categorized into several types, primarily including oil-immersed transformers, dry-type transformers, and others. Oil-immersed transformers operate by submerging components in insulating oil, providing cooling and insulation, making them suitable for high-power applications. Dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling and insulation, ideal for indoor usage due to reduced fire risk. Other types encompass specialized transformers like natural ester-filled and custom designs, addressing varied industrial needs and enhancing energy efficiency across applications.</p></p>
