Automatic discharge magnet controllers facilitate the automatic release of ferromagnetic materials, enhancing operational efficiency and safety. These controllers often include features like timers and sensors to ensure precise operation. In contrast, lifting magnet controllers are designed for managing the lifting and positioning of heavy metals in industrial settings, allowing for real-time adjustments and increased control during lifting operations, ensuring smoother workflows and reducing manual labor.</p></p>
