<p><p>The oil and gas milling equipment market encompasses various types of mills used for machining processes in the sector. End mills are versatile tools for creating complex shapes, while roughing end mills remove material efficiently. Slab mills are ideal for flat surfaces, and thread mills create internal threads. Hollow mills form cylindrical shapes, while shell mills are used for large openings. Cement mills prepare materials in drilling operations, and taper mills create angled surfaces essential for precision tasks in oil and gas applications.</p></p>
