<p><p>Self-polishing antifouling paint is a specialized marine coating designed to prevent the accumulation of marine organisms on the submerged surfaces of vessels. Copper-free paints utilize alternative biocides or eco-friendly additives to prevent marine growth, making them suitable for environmentally sensitive areas. Tin-free paints, on the other hand, do not contain harmful organotin compounds and are designed to gradually release active ingredients as they wear away, inhibiting fouling while posing less risk to marine life. Both types aim to enhance vessel performance and reduce maintenance needs.</p></p>
