<p><p>The Plexiglass sheet market is primarily divided into two types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are made by forcing acrylic resin through a die, resulting in thinner, lightweight sheets that are ideal for applications like signs and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are produced by polymerizing liquid acrylic between two glass plates, creating thicker, more durable sheets that offer superior optical clarity, making them suitable for use in high-end applications like furniture and safety glazing.</p></p>
