<p><p>The higher fatty alcohol market is categorized into short-chain, long-chain, and higher-chain segments based on the carbon chain length. Short-chain fatty alcohols typically contain 1 to 4 carbons and are used in applications like cosmetics and detergents. Long-chain fatty alcohols, comprising 12 to 22 carbons, are utilized in lubricants, surfactants, and personal care products. Higher-chain fatty alcohols, which have more than 22 carbons, are primarily used in specialty applications such as high-performance lubricants and industrial formulations.</p></p>
