<p><p>The processed glass market includes several key types: laminated glass, toughened glass, and others. Laminated glass consists of layers bonded with interlayers, enhancing safety and security by holding shards together when broken. Toughened glass is heat-treated to increase strength and thermal resistance, making it ideal for high-stress applications. Other types encompass various specialty glasses, such as patterned, coated, or insulated glass, catering to diverse industrial and architectural needs while contributing to energy efficiency and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
