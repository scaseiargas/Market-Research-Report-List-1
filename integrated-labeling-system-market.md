<p><p>The Integrated Labeling System market comprises three main types: manual labeling, semi-automatic labeling, and automatic labeling. Manual labeling involves human workforce to apply labels, ideal for low-volume production. Semi-automatic labeling combines manual effort with some machine assistance, enhancing efficiency for medium-volume needs. Automatic labeling systems fully automate the labeling process, offering high speed and precision, suitable for large-scale production. Each type caters to different production demands, ensuring flexibility in labeling solutions across various industries.</p></p>
