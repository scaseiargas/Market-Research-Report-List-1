<p><p>The PV power station market is divided into two primary types: on-grid and off-grid systems. On-grid PV power stations are connected to the electrical grid, allowing for the sale of surplus energy back to the utility, enhancing energy reliability and reducing costs. Off-grid PV power stations operate independently, providing power to remote locations where grid access is unavailable, often using battery systems for energy storage. Both markets cater to different energy needs and preferences, promoting renewable energy adoption.</p></p>
