<p><p>Chilled water buffer tanks are essential in HVAC systems for maintaining balanced chilled water supply and demand. The vertical type features a tall, cylindrical design, maximizing space efficiency in smaller areas and typically having a smaller footprint. Conversely, horizontal tanks have a wider base, suitable for larger installations where height restrictions exist. Both types serve to enhance system efficiency, reduce pump cycling, and improve temperature stability by storing chilled water during low-demand periods for use during peak loads.</p></p>
