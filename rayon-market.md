<p><p>The rayon market is primarily categorized into two types: viscose staple fiber and viscose filament fiber. Viscose staple fiber is used for producing textiles, home furnishings, and non-woven fabrics, known for its softness and absorbency. In contrast, viscose filament fiber is utilized in high-end fabrics, offering a luxurious feel and luster, often found in apparel and upholstery. Both types capitalize on natural cellulose and are favored for their versatility and eco-friendly properties, catering to diverse market demands.</p></p>
