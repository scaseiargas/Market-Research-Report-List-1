<p><p>The ink and toner cartridges market is primarily divided into two main types: ink cartridges and toner cartridges. Ink cartridges are designed for inkjet printers, utilizing liquid ink to produce high-quality color and detail in printouts, making them ideal for photos and graphics. In contrast, toner cartridges are used in laser printers and contain powdered toner, delivering faster printing speeds, greater efficiency, and sharper text quality, making them suitable for high-volume office environments. Both types cater to different printing needs and technologies.</p></p>
