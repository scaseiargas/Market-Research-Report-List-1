<p><p>The endometrial ablation equipment market encompasses various techniques used to treat abnormal uterine bleeding by destroying the endometrial lining. Radiofrequency ablation employs heat to remove tissue, while cryoablation uses extreme cold. Hydrothermal ablation utilizes heated fluid, while thermal balloon involves inflating a balloon filled with heated fluid to ablate tissue. Hysteroscopic ablation combines visualization and direct ablation within the uterus. Other methods may include laser or microwave ablation. Each technique varies in approach, effectiveness, and patient recovery time.</p></p>
