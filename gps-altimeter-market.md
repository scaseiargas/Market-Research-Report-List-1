<p><p>The GPS altimeter is an advanced device that determines altitude by utilizing signals from Global Positioning System (GPS) satellites. Unlike traditional altimeters that measure altitude based on atmospheric pressure, GPS altimeters provide more accurate readings by calculating the distance from satellites. This technology is particularly valuable in various applications, including aviation, outdoor activities, and various military and commercial uses.</p><p>The GPS Altimeter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for precise altitude measurement in multiple sectors. Additionally, the rising trend of outdoor activities, such as hiking and adventure sports, is fueling the demand for portable GPS devices equipped with altimeters. </p><p>The market is also witnessing innovations, such as the combination of GPS altimeters with other technologies like barometers and inertial navigations systems for enhanced accuracy and reliability.
