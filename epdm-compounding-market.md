<p><p>The EPDM compounding market is categorized into three main types: Block Type, Particles/Crumb Type, and Powder Type. The Block Type consists of larger, solid EPDM sections, ideal for applications requiring significant structural integrity. Particles/Crumb Type features smaller granules, suitable for easier blending and processing in various applications. Powder Type, on the other hand, is finely ground EPDM, perfect for achieving a uniform distribution in coatings and adhesives. Each type serves distinct industrial needs and processing methods in the EPDM landscape.</p></p>
