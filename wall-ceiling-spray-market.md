<p><p>The Wall & Ceiling Spray market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into water-based and water-resistant sprays. Water-based sprays are primarily composed of water, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. They typically dry quickly and emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-resistant sprays are designed to repel moisture, providing added protection in humid environments. These sprays often contain additives that enhance durability, making them suitable for areas prone to dampness, such as bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
