<p><p>Weather-proof glass cement is categorized into two main types based on their chemical properties: acid and neutral. Acid weather-proof glass cement typically has a lower pH and is more aggressive in bonding, making it suitable for specific applications where strong adhesion is required. In contrast, neutral weather-proof glass cement has a balanced pH, making it less reactive. This type is ideal for applications where compatibility with various materials is necessary, ensuring versatility and reducing the risk of chemical damage to surfaces.</p></p>
