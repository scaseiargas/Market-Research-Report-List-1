<p><ul><li>Vortex Shedding Flowmeter</li><li>Vane/Piston Flowmeter</li><li>Paddlewheel Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical flow meter market includes various technologies, such as vortex shedding flowmeters, vane/piston flowmeters, and paddlewheel flowmeters. Vortex shedding flowmeters operate by measuring the frequency of vortices shed by an obstruction in the flow, suitable for various fluids. Vane/piston flowmeters utilize moving parts to gauge flow rates, often used in applications requiring precise measurement. Paddlewheel flowmeters employ a rotating paddle to indicate flow speed, known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for water and low-viscosity fluids.</p></p>
