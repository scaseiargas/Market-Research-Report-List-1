<p><p>The release film market is categorized into organic and inorganic types based on material composition. Organic release films are made from polymers like silicone, polyethylene, or polypropylene, offering flexibility, durability, and compatibility with various substrates. They are widely used in industries like electronics and composites. Inorganic release films, typically composed of materials like ceramics or metals, provide high thermal and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Both types serve as protective layers, enabling easy separation of materials during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
