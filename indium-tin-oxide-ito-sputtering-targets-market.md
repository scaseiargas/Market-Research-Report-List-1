<p><p>The Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) sputtering targets market consists mainly of two types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets feature a flat surface and are commonly used for applications requiring precise coating over small areas, making them ideal for laboratory research and small-scale production. Rotary targets, on the other hand, are cylindrical and allow for high-efficiency deposition over larger substrates, making them suitable for mass production in display and photovoltaic applications. Both types play a crucial role in thin-film technology.</p></p>
