<p><ul><li>Torch Brazing</li><li>Induction Brazing</li><li>Furnace Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum brazing sheets market encompasses various brazing techniques such as torch brazing, induction brazing, and furnace brazing. Torch brazing involves using an open flame to melt filler material and join aluminum parts, offering flexibility for on-site applications. Induction brazing utilizes electromagnetic induction to heat the metal, enabling precise and efficient connections in industrial settings. Furnace brazing, performed in controlled environments, is ideal for high-volume production, ensuring uniform heating and minimizing oxidation, making it suitable for automotive and HVAC applications.</p></p>
