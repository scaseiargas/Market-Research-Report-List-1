<p><p>The osteosynthesis products market comprises two main types: degradable and nondegradable materials. Degradable materials, such as biocompatible polymers, gradually dissolve in the body, promoting natural healing and eliminating the need for removal surgery. In contrast, nondegradable materials, including stainless steel and titanium, provide long-lasting support for fractures but require surgical intervention for removal after healing. Both types play crucial roles in orthopedic surgeries, catering to different patient needs and preferences in bone fixation and healing processes.</p></p>
