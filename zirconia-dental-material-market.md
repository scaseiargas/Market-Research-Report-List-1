<p><p>The zirconia dental material market primarily includes zirconia dental discs and zirconia dental blocks. Zirconia dental discs are thin, circular pieces of zirconia used in CAD/CAM systems for fabricating crowns and bridges, offering durability and aesthetic appeal. Zirconia dental blocks, on the other hand, are thicker and provide more versatility in various dental applications, including large restorations and custom implants. Both types are favored for their strength, biocompatibility, and excellent aesthetic properties, making them ideal for modern dental practices.</p></p>
