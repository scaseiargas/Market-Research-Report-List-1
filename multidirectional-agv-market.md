<p><p>The multidirectional Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market encompasses various guiding technologies, primarily optical and magnetic guiding systems. Optical guiding AGVs utilize visual signals, such as lasers or reflective markers, to navigate their environment, offering flexibility and adaptability to dynamic layouts. In contrast, magnetic guiding AGVs rely on magnetic strips or sensors embedded in the floor, providing a reliable and straightforward navigation method. Both types enhance operational efficiency, optimize material handling, and support automation across industries like manufacturing and warehousing.</p></p>
