<p><ul><li>Silica Gel White Desiccant</li><li>Silica Gel Blue Desiccant</li><li>Silica Gel Orange Desiccant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food packaging silica gel desiccants market consists of three primary types: white, blue, and orange silica gel. White silica gel is non-indicator and effectively absorbs moisture, making it a popular choice for general use in food packaging. Blue silica gel contains moisture indicators that change color when saturated, signaling the need for replacement. Orange silica gel is similar in function but uses a different color-changing indicator. Each type is vital for preserving food quality by preventing spoilage and extending shelf life.</p></p>
