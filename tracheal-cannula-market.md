<p><p>The tracheal cannula market is primarily divided into two main types: silicone tracheal cannulas and PVC tracheal cannulas. Silicone tracheal cannulas are known for their flexibility, biocompatibility, and durability, making them suitable for long-term use. They are often favored in patients requiring extended ventilation. PVC tracheal cannulas, on the other hand, are cost-effective and widely used in short-term applications. They offer adequate ventilation support but may not be as comfortable for prolonged wear. Both types serve vital roles in respiratory management.</p></p>
