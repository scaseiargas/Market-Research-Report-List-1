<p><p>The magnesium-aluminum alloy market encompasses two primary types: "Rolled Gold for Thin and Thick Plates" and "Extruded Alloy." Rolled gold products are processed into flat sheets of varying thicknesses, suitable for applications requiring lightweight yet strong materials, often used in aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, extruded alloys involve forcing molten metal through a die, producing long shapes like bars and profiles, ideal for structural applications and components, offering versatility and enhanced mechanical properties.</p></p>
