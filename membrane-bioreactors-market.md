<p><ul><li>CCAS Processing Technology</li><li>Continuous Microfiltration Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane Bioreactors (MBRs) are categorized primarily into two market types: CCAS Processing Technology and Continuous Microfiltration Technology. CCAS (Continuous Controlled Aeration System) utilizes controlled aeration to enhance biological treatment and membrane filtration efficiency, making it suitable for various wastewater applications. Continuous Microfiltration Technology, on the other hand, employs a constant flow of feed water through microfiltration membranes, ensuring effective separation of solids and microorganisms. Both technologies contribute to improved water quality and resource recovery, addressing the growing demand for sustainable wastewater management solutions.</p></p>
