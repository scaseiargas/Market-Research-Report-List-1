Visible Fluorescence Imaging Systems utilize visible light to enhance tissue visualization during endoscopic procedures, helping to identify abnormalities effectively. In contrast, Near-Infrared Fluorescence Imaging Systems employ near-infrared light, offering deeper tissue penetration and improved contrast, which is beneficial for detecting tumors and vascular structures. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and surgical outcomes in various medical applications.</p></p>
