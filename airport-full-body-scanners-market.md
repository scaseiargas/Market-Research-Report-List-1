<p><strong>The Airport Full Body Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport full body scanner market primarily consists of two types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize ionizing radiation to create detailed images of passengers, allowing security personnel to identify concealed items quickly. In contrast, millimeter wave scanners employ non-ionizing radio waves to generate 3D images, detecting objects beneath clothing without revealing personal details. Both technologies enhance airport security by improving threat detection while minimizing wait times for travelers. Each type has distinct advantages and applications in various airport settings.</p></p>
