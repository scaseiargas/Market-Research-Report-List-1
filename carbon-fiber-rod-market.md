<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Rod Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber Rod</li><li>Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Rod market includes PAN-based and Pitch-based types. PAN-based carbon fiber rods are made from polyacrylonitrile, offering high strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties, making them ideal for aerospace, automotive, and sports industries. Pitch-based carbon fiber rods, derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch, excel in thermal conductivity and stiffness, often used in high-temperature applications like electronics and industrial equipment. Both types cater to specialized needs, with PAN-based focusing on structural performance and Pitch-based on thermal and electrical applications.</p></p>
