<p><strong>The Linear Pneumatic Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Acting</li><li>Single Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear pneumatic cylinder market comprises two main types: double-acting and single-acting cylinders. Double-acting cylinders utilize air pressure for both extending and retracting movements, offering greater versatility and control in applications. In contrast, single-acting cylinders use air pressure for extension only, relying on a spring or external force for retraction, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. These cylinders are widely used in automation, manufacturing, and material handling industries, catering to different operational needs and space considerations.</p></p>
