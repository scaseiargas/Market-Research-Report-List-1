<p><strong>The FTTx Fiber Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FTTx fiber optic cables encompass various deployment types, primarily single-mode and multi-mode fibers. Single-mode fiber features a narrow core, allowing light to travel straight down the fiber, which supports long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-mode fiber has a larger core, enabling multiple light paths; it is ideal for shorter distances and lower costs. Both types cater to different applications in telecommunications and internet infrastructure, facilitating high-speed data transfer in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
