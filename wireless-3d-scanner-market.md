<p><p>The Wireless 3D Scanner market primarily consists of two types: Laser and Structured Light scanners. Laser scanners use laser beams to capture precise distance measurements, creating accurate 3D models, ideal for large-scale objects and outdoor environments. In contrast, Structured Light scanners project patterns of light onto the object, capturing surface details through image processing, making them suitable for smaller, intricate items. Both technologies are essential for various applications, including manufacturing, architecture, and quality control.</p></p>
