<p><strong>The Near Field Communication in Healthcare Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reader/Writer Mode</li><li>Card Emulation Mode</li><li>Peer-to-Peer Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) in healthcare consists of three main market types. In Reader/Writer Mode, healthcare devices, like tablets or smartphones, read data from NFC-enabled tags attached to patient records or medication. Card Emulation Mode allows NFC-enabled devices to function like smart cards, enabling secure access to electronic health records or payment systems. In Peer-to-Peer Mode, two NFC devices can exchange data, facilitating fast and secure information sharing between medical staff or between patients and providers, enhancing overall healthcare delivery.</p></p>
