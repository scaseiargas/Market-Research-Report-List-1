<p><p>Commercial Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) are optical devices that enable the selective filtering of specific wavelengths of light using sound waves. The market is primarily segmented into collinear and noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs align the acoustic and optical beams in the same direction, offering high efficiency and compact design for applications in spectroscopy and telecommunications. In contrast, noncollinear AOTFs operate with the beams at an angle, providing broader tuning ranges and enhanced spectral resolution, suitable for diverse imaging and monitoring applications.</p></p>
