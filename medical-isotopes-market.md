<p><p>The medical isotopes market consists of two primary types: stable isotopes and radioisotopes. Stable isotopes are non-radioactive and are used in various applications, including diagnostics and research. They provide valuable insights in molecular biology and biochemistry. In contrast, radioisotopes are radioactive and are predominantly utilized in diagnostic imaging and therapeutic procedures, such as cancer treatment. Both types play crucial roles in modern medicine, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and treatment efficacy while contributing to advancements in medical research.</p></p>
