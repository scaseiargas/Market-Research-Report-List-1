<p><p>The One Time Password (OTP) display card market is categorized into two main types: OTP magnetic cards and OTP chip cards. OTP magnetic cards utilize a magnetic stripe to store and display dynamic passwords, providing a physical device for secure access to sensitive information. In contrast, OTP chip cards incorporate advanced microchip technology, enhancing security through encryption and tamper resistance. Both types aim to protect online transactions and authentication processes, offering users enhanced security in a digital landscape increasingly vulnerable to cyber threats.</p></p>
