<p><p>The Copper Litz Wire market comprises two main types: Directly Bunched Type and Multi-step Bunched Type. Directly Bunched Type features strands tightly grouped together, reducing losses due to skin effect, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, Multi-step Bunched Type involves multiple layers of wire strands twisted in a specific configuration, enhancing flexibility and performance in various environments. Both types are utilized in electrical applications, optimizing efficiency by minimizing resistance and improving overall conductivity.</p></p>
