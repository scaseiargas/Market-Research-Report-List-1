<p><p>The endodontic explorer market is categorized into two primary types: straight and curved explorers. Straight explorers feature a linear design, allowing for easy access to straight canals and precise detection of canal orifices. In contrast, curved explorers are designed to navigate the intricate anatomy of curved or irregularly shaped canals, enhancing the dentist's ability to locate and treat difficult areas effectively. Both types serve essential roles in endodontic procedures, improving treatment outcomes and patient care.</p></p>
