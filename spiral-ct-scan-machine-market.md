<p><p>The Spiral CT Scan Machine market is segmented into two main types: Double Layer Helical CT Scanners and Multi-Layer Helical CT Scanners. Double Layer Helical CT Scanners utilize two detector rows to capture images quickly, enhancing speed and image quality for various diagnostic applications. In contrast, Multi-Layer Helical CT Scanners employ multiple detector rows, enabling better spatial resolution and faster scanning times. Both technologies are crucial for improving diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in medical imaging, catering to varying clinical needs.</p></p>
