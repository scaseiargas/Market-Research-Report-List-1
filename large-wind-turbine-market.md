<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large wind turbine market primarily consists of two types: horizontal-axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs are the most common, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, making them efficient for capturing wind energy at varying heights. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, allowing them to harness wind from any direction and operate in turbulent conditions. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different applications and site conditions in renewable energy production.</p></p>
