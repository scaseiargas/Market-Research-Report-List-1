<p><strong>The Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) market comprises different types based on their powertrain configurations. In series plug-in hybrids, the electric motor primarily drives the vehicle, while the internal combustion engine acts as a generator to recharge the battery. In parallel hybrids, both the electric motor and the combustion engine can drive the wheels simultaneously or independently. The hybrid plug-in market combines elements of both, offering flexibility and efficiency by allowing drivers to switch between electric and gasoline power based on conditions.</p></p>
