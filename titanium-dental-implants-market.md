<p><p>The Titanium Dental Implants market comprises three main types: Endosteal implants, Subperiosteal implants, and others. Endosteal implants are placed directly into the jawbone, providing a sturdy foundation for artificial teeth. Subperiosteal implants, on the other hand, rest on top of the jawbone but beneath the gum tissue, ideal for patients with insufficient bone height. Other types may include zygomatic and mini implants, catering to specialized patient needs. Together, these options enhance dental restoration possibilities for various clinical scenarios.</p></p>
